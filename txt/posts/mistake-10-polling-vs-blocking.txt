  SPECIAL -- IPC MISTAKE: POLLING VS BLOCKING
  Mistake 10: Polling vs Blocking
  ============================================================================================


      Using a standard file for real-time IPC requires the consumer to spin in an infinite loop (`while(true) read()`), which burns 100% CPU (polling). Real IPC needs blocking primitives (like sockets or pipes) that put the thread to sleep until data arrives.
