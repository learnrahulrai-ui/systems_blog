  SPECIAL -- IPC MISTAKE: FILE DESCRIPTOR LEAK EMFILE
  Mistake 15: File Descriptor Leak EMFILE
  ============================================================================================


  Forgetting to `close()` the socket after handling a client permanently leaks the file descriptor integer (e.g., 4, 5, 6...). The process eventually hits the OS limit and `accept()` fails with `EMFILE`. FDs are process-local and free instantly on close.
